SAMWAD

Commemorating the role of women in the fields of sciences, technology, engineering and mathematics and elevating their contribution in these domains by catering to their needs, providing them with opportunities and felicitating the meritorious, IEEE celebrates the eternal legacy of the women’s role in engineering by organizingan event called WIE. The paramount aim of WIE is exalting the role of women in engineering. "Knowledge shared is knowledge squared” Focuses on uplifting the underprivileged. Appreciates the women in engineering. IEEE MSB every year orchestrates an event “SAMWAD“ under WIE, to realize the indomitable spirit of women and to praise and promote their significant contribution.
